We announce the launch of the S&P/B3 IBOVESPA VIX Index, by ticker code VXBR, today, March 19, 2024.

This index is a measure of the volatility of the Brazilian market considering its calculation linked between the first two months of the index options' maturities plus the DI rate.

The calculation and publication of this index is the responsibility of S&P, its calculation takes place between 10:30 am and 4:45 pm with publication every 15 seconds.

We provide below the URL link below for more details about this index.

https://www.spglobal.com/spdji/pt/indices/indicators/sp-b3-ibovespa-vix/#overview

Finally, we highlight that this index is configured for publication in the Market Data UMDF FIX/FAST feed through channel 64.
