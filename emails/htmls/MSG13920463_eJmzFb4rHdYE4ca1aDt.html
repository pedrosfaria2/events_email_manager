We hereby inform you that on March 04, 2024, the last wave related to Binary Market Data maintenance release will be implemented, encompassing an update of the templates file for channels 74, 72, 78, 84, 88 and 90 and the changes described below.

1. Change to the number of decimal places in the “minPriceIncrement” and “tradingReferencePrice” fields for Economic Indicators

Economic Indicators are non-negotiable instruments and one of their characteristics is the publication of values with more decimal places than is defined in the Market Data templates file.

2. Removal of fields
The indexPct (6919) and indexTheoreticalQty (37021) fields will be removed from the SecurityDefinition message to reduce the size of the messages.

We highlight that the update of the new templates file will be mandatory, with deactivation of the publication of the previous versions of the messages in accordance with the tranches plan.

The 1.7.0 version of the templates file is available for validation in a certification environment since January 15, 2024 .

While B3 recommends testing systems in a non-productive environment, it is not mandatory that the currently certified systems undergo a new certification process.

This maintenance release corrects all the Binary Market Data known issues, published at https://clientes.b3.com.br/en_us/, Roadmap, typing “Binary Market Data” in the Advanced Filter field, then clicking on Binary Market Data, Attachments, Binary Market Data - Known Issues v1.0.
