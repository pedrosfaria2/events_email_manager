We hereby inform you that the expiration of weekly options will occur today (March 01, 2024).

In this sense, we highlight the timetable valid for automatic options exercise.

  • From 10:00 a.m. to 03:45 p.m. - Period for manual exercise of holder's position.
  • From 10:00 a.m. to 04:45 p.m. - Trading in expiring options (there will not be closing call for expiring options).
  • Until 05:45 p.m. - Allocation/Give-up
  • At 06:15 p.m. - Receiving of positions file. 
  • From 06:40 p.m. to 07:10 p.m. - Order cancellation phase.
  • From 08:15 p.m. to 07:15 p.m. - Window to request the contrary exercise.
  • At 07:15 p.m. - Triggering of the automatic exercise of holder's position in the money + execution of the contrary exercise requests.
  • From 07:25 p.m. to 07:40 p.m. - After-market trading
  • Until 08:00 p.m. - Corrections.
  • From 07:45 p.m. to 08:00 p.m. - Order cancellation phase.

The publication of the Price files (BVBG.086) and the Simplified Trading Bulletins (BVBG.186 and BVBG.187) on the expiration date of options on shares, units and ETFs (weekly and monthly), will occur at approximately the same time as 8:15 pm.

We also emphasize that options will be automatically exercised by B3 in line with the following conditions:

  • For call options, if the reference price is higher than the strike;
  • For put options, if the reference price is lower than the strike;
  • If a demand is not received for the contrary exercise not to be exercised when it is an in-the-money option;
  • When an out-of-the-money or an at-the-money option receives a contrary exercise request to be exercised and the underlying is within the price tunnel or under B3 analysis.

The timetable for expiring series trading, manual exercise, automatic exercise, contrary exercise and for the after-market is available at http://www.b3.com.br/en_us, Solutions, Platforms, PUMA Trading System, Participants and traders, Trading hours.
