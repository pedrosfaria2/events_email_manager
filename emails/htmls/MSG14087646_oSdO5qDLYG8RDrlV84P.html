We would like to inform you that, as of the first preview of the May portfolio (April 1st 2024), the weighting of economic groups that have more than one asset (Common and Preferred Shares) will be carried out based on the Free Float of each asset, and, due to that, the company's Diversity Score will be divided proportionally between these assets.

Example: suppose that company A has a Diversity Score of 35. If it was represented in the index by ON and PN shares, the Diversity Score was assigned to both ON and PN. To avoid this duplicity, the Diversity Score, from now on, will be proportional to the Free Float (number of shares in circulation) of each asset. If the ON share represents 30% of the company's Free Float Market Cap, the Diversity Score considered for weighting would be 10.5 (i.e., 30% of 35). Consequently, the PN share would count a Diversity Score of 24.5 (70% of 35) for weighting.
