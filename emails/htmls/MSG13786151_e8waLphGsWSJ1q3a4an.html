Seeking the continuous improvement of our trading platform, we inform you that, from monday February 26, 2024 , there will be a change in behavior in sending Execution Reports for cancellations of GTC/GTD orders arising from corporate events that occur exclusively on the last business day of the week when the gateways are no longer active.
 
This measure is required, given that currently, there is a specific scenario in which when a corporate action on the last business day of the week and the gateways are no longer active, the Execution Report messages canceling GTC/GTD orders (for contracts affected by this action) are not being received by our customers
 
In this sense, the new behavior will be to send all Execution Report messages regarding cancellations of GTC/GTD orders, arising from this specific scenario, only on the next business day when the gateways are active.

Further information can be obtained from Trading Support by telephone on +55 11 2565-5021 or by email at tradingsupport@b3.com.br.
