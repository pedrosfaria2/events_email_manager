In this sense, in order to provide more details about the scenario of multiple STOP orders triggered with the same price executing the Book, an ExecutionSummary block was sent for each attacking order

In this version, a single ExecutionSummary block is sent, containing all the trades generated from the trigger, making it possible to have different aggressor orders, since all other STOP orders are due to the same initial event. This behavior prevents the false perception of the final state of processing orders in the Matching Engine, preventing the market from reacting to intermediate scenarios.

Before update

After update
  • Book update messages published only at the end of all event processing, generating incomplete ExecutionSummary blocks.
  • Trades on aggressor STOP orders in each block of ExecutionSummary.
  • Book update messages published within your ExecutionSummary blocks.
  • Trades with multiple aggressive STOPs orders grouped in each ExecutionSummary block, reducing the number of messages on the network and the chance of the market taking actions in intermediate states of the Matching Engine.

We hightlight that this change was applied and finished in Derivatives segment on December 11, 2024
