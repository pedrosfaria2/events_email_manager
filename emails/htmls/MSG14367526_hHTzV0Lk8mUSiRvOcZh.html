We hereby inform you that a business continuity test of the PUMA Trading System will ne held on Saturday, May 11, 2023, by means of a mock trading session in the contingency environment in order to validate:

• Connectivity between brokerage houses’ primary site facilities and B3’s backup infrastructure; and

• Execution of the mandatory test script at B3’s alternative site. 

The addresses for connection between B3’s backup servers and participants must be properly configured and enabled.The connectivity tables are available at www.b3.com.br/en_us/, Solutions, Platforms, PUMA Trading System:

• EntryPoint FIX/FAST – Order Entry FIX, IPs List, Entrypoint - IP address list ;

• Market Data FIX/FAST, UMDF Channels Definition – BOVESPA Segment and BM&F Segment;

• Market Data Binary - Binary UMDF, Definition of channel multicast addresses Binary UMDF;

• EntryPoint Binary – Binary Order Entry, IPs list, Binary Order Entry - IP List – Production and DR - SBEOE Gateway v1 ;

• DropCopy - Order Entry FIX, IPs List, Production - Connectivity Details ,

The connection to the ePUMA trading screen must made on ports 5050 and 7050. Market participants who do not have this configuration set in the application, must request an updated channels.xml file to the Trading Support team, by e-mail tradingsupport@b3.com.br, with the subject “Channels ePUMA”.

The entire test window is programmed to take place between 01:00 p.m. and 3:00 p.m. Participation by brokerage houses, market data distributors and independent software vendors operating on Listed B3 is mandatory.

2. Scope of mock trading session

The test scripts for the mock trading session in the contingency environment are enclosed on this message and will cover the following:

• Reception of market data via Feed C;

• Trading (order entry and trade execution) in accordance with the mandatory script

• Free mock trading (order entry and trade execution)

• Order cancellation via admin session

• Validation of trading limits (LiNe 5.0), using the limits in effect at the end of the trading session on May 10, 2024 (Friday)

• Management of trading limits (LiNe 5.0)

• Reception of execution messages generated by trading systems

• Reception of order registration, modification and cancellation message drop copies and execution reports

• Use of Firmsoft order cancellation software • Reception of electronic trading news 

3. Morning test of electronic trading system

Morning test of electronic trading system Because the mock trading session will use B3’s contingency addresses and infrastructure, the morning test on May 13, 2024 (Monday) will be held exceptionally from 7:00 a.m. to 8:00 a.m. for Listed B3 to allow participants to validate the return of their configurations to the infrastructure address at B3’s main site.

Participation by all brokerage houses in this morning test is mandatory.

Other information

Participation in the mock trading session must be confirmed no later than 12 noon on May 10, 2024 (Friday), by emailing tradingsupport@b3.com.br, with the subject line “Business Continuity Test”, identifying the institution, naming the individuals who will participate ,and supplying their contact details (telephone numbers and email addresses). In response to this confirmation, participants will receive the test script, and must fill it out and send it to the above email addresses.
