We inform you that as established in Circular Letter 052/2024-PRE, dated 04/11/2024, to assess compliance with the minimum margin in the Bitcoin Futures Contract, the monitoring of the percentage indicator of zeroing actions by brokers occurring in up to 30 seconds.

Until May 20, 2024, participants who have already adopted the “ZAS” sender location will have the monitoring process carried out as described below, while the other participants will be monitored in accordance with the process defined in Circular Letter 052/2024-PRE of 04/11/2024.

As of May 20, 2024, B3 will no longer monitor only resets carried out via sender location “ZER” and will start monitoring two indicators, as follows:

1. Zeroing Indicator by sender location “ZER”: indicator of compulsory zeroing, that is, only when the broker, in its responsibility for controlling and managing risk and through its risk area, reverses or reduces the position of its client when the loss limit stipulated by the broker itself is reached.

2. Zeroing Indicator by sender location “ZAS”: indicator of zeroings carried out systematically by the broker based on parameters defined by the investor himself.

To calculate each of the indicators of zeroing actions by brokers, the following procedures will be carried out monthly, as part of the monitoring to be carried out by B3:
the following procedures:
(i) identification of actions with reduction or complete liquidation of the position by the broker through orders using the sender location “ZER” and the sender location “ZAS”, individually;
(ii) identification of the time of the broker's action: time at which the broker's action occurred, whether to reduce or completely liquidate the position;
(iii) identification of the average time for assembling the position: by calculating the duration weighted by the quantity of each trade during the assembling of the position;
(iv) definition of the duration of the position by subtracting the broker's operating hours from the average position setting time;
(v) counting the total number of broker actions for each sender location (for reduction or complete liquidation of the position);
(vi) count of how many broker actions occurred within 30 seconds (to reduce or completely liquidate the position) for each sender location;
(vii) dividing the actions that occurred within 30 seconds, inclusive, by the total number of actions for each sender location.

If one of the two monthly indicators (for “ZER” or “ZAS”) exceeds the level of 5.0% of zeros, calculated to one decimal place, as long as the number of actions by the broker in up to 30 seconds is greater than 100 in the month, B3 will begin the sanction procedure set out in the B3 Trading Operational Procedures Manual.
B3 will calculate, on a daily basis, the zeroing indicators by the sender location “ZER” and by the sender location “ZAS”, individually, and will disclose them, to each participant, both in the daily view and in the accumulated view for the month.
With this, participants will be able to monitor their indicators and take the actions they deem necessary throughout the month.
