Meeting the demand of market participants, from today, January 29, 2024, Weekly Options Contracts on Shares, Units and ETFs will be available for trading.

We highlight the main features of the product below:

Main benefits:

Greater flexibility and precision in executing strategies;
Shorter exposure time to risk (due to the shorter time to maturity);
Lower premiums for having exposure to the same amount of underlying asset vs. Monthly options.

Weekly due date rule: every Friday of the month with the exception of the 3rd Friday of the month, as this is the existing monthly due date.

1st expiration date available on the launch date:  This will occur, exceptionally in the second week of the month (02/09/2024) to facilitate the market's adaptation to this new product.

Ticker Structure: AAAABCCCWn, in which the only new feature in relation to the monthly options ticker is the addition of 2 characters at the end, with “W” indicating that it is a weekly option and “n” indicating which Friday -Friday of the month the weekly option expires (e.g.: n=1 refers to expiration on the 1st Friday of the month of expiration)

Additionally, there will be no “W3” ending, as the 3rd Friday of the month is the monthly option expiration.
