We inform that a new version of the document is available CID (Customer Impact Document) “UMDF FIXFAST and Conflated – Platform Update”, available at HTTPS://WWW.B3.COM.BR, Solutions, Platforms, Puma Trading System, For developers and vendors, Market Data FIX/FAST, UMDF and UMDF Conflated.

 

Highlights the addition of the item relating to news messages (35=B) generated by news agencies on channel 63, which were also redundantly replicated on non-news derivatives channels. News messages generated by news agencies remain unchanged, being published on channel 63 (for Equities and Derivatives), but will no longer be replicated redundantly on derivatives channels. There is also no change for news messages generated by the Matching Engine, they continue to be published directly on the Equities and Derivatives channels. The aforementioned behavior only applies to UMDF, since we do not have this replication in UMDF Conflated.

 

We also emphasize that the changes are available for testing and validation in the certification environment.
